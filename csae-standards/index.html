SK APPS Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE) Prevention Standards

Last updated: April 7, 2026

🚫 Zero Tolerance Policy

SK APPS has a strict zero-tolerance policy toward Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE) and Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM). We are committed to providing safe environments for all users.

Any content, behavior, or activity involving child sexual abuse or exploitation is strictly prohibited across all SK APPS applications. Violations result in immediate account termination and reporting to law enforcement authorities.

📋 Prohibited Content & Behavior

The following are expressly prohibited in all SK APPS applications:

  • Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM): Any image, video, text, or other content that sexually depicts, exploits, or abuses minors
  • Grooming: Any attempt to establish inappropriate relationships with minors for exploitation
  • Solicitation: Requesting sexual content from or initiating sexual contact with minors
  • Trafficking: Any content or behavior facilitating trafficking of children
  • Exploitation: Any form of child exploitation, abuse, or endangerment

🔍 Detection & Prevention Measures

SK APPS implements proactive measures to prevent CSAE across our platforms:

  • Content Moderation: User-generated content is subject to review and moderation
  • In-App Reporting: All apps with user-generated content include an in-app reporting mechanism for child safety concerns
  • Account Suspension: Accounts found to violate child safety policies are immediately suspended
  • Data Retention: Evidence related to child safety violations is preserved and provided to law enforcement upon lawful request

📢 How to Report Child Safety Concerns

If you encounter any content or behavior that you believe involves child sexual abuse or exploitation, please report it immediately:

In-App Reporting: Use the "Report" button available within the app to flag concerning content or behavior.

Email Report: Send details to app.develop.sk@gmail.com with subject line "CHILD SAFETY REPORT". Include as much detail as possible.

We review all child safety reports with urgency and will take immediate action, including reporting to the appropriate authorities when required by law.

⚖️ Legal Compliance & Reporting to Authorities

SK APPS complies with all applicable laws regarding child safety, including:

  • NCMEC: We report known CSAM to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) CyberTipline as required by US law (18 U.S.C. § 2258A)
  • Local Authorities: We cooperate fully with law enforcement agencies in all jurisdictions where our apps operate
  • IWF: We work with the Internet Watch Foundation and similar organizations to identify and remove CSAM
  • GDPR/Privacy: While we protect user privacy, child safety takes absolute priority — we will share data with authorities when legally required

📞 Dedicated Child Safety Contact

Our dedicated contact for child safety, CSAM prevention, and compliance inquiries is available at all times:

Subject line: "CHILD SAFETY" or "CSAM REPORT"

This contact can explain our CSAM prevention measures and compliance status at any time.

🔄 Policy Updates

This policy is reviewed and updated regularly to reflect changes in applicable laws and best practices. Material changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.
