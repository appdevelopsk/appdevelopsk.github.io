Privacy Policy

Reset Loop — Last updated: April 20, 2026

Information We Collect

Reset Loop collects your app usage data (daily screen time per commitment) and commitment settings (app name, daily limit in minutes, donation amount per trigger) that you configure within the app. This data is stored on our servers and linked to an anonymous device identifier.

How We Use Your Information

Usage data and commitment settings are used to calculate daily limits, generate weekly reports, and display community-level aggregated statistics (total donation amounts, participant counts). Individual data is never publicly disclosed.

Community Transparency

Reset Loop displays aggregated community statistics such as total planned donations and number of participants. These figures are computed from anonymized data and do not reveal any individual user's information.

Analytics

We use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymized usage data (screen views, feature usage) to improve the app. No personally identifiable information is collected.

Advertising

The app displays ads via Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers for ad personalization in accordance with Google's privacy policy.

Data Retention

Your commitment settings and usage logs are stored on our servers as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your data by contacting us.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Contact us at app.develop.sk@gmail.com
