Privacy Policy

Idea Market — Last updated: April 16, 2026

Information We Collect

Idea Market collects the following information when you use the app: your display name, posted business ideas, and transaction history. This data is stored on our servers to provide the marketplace functionality.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to display your ideas on the marketplace, process transactions, and provide the core app features. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Analytics

We use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymized usage data (screen views, feature usage) to improve the app. No personally identifiable information is collected.

Advertising

Free users see ads via Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers for ad personalization in accordance with Google's privacy policy. Premium users see no ads.

Data Retention

Your account data and posted ideas are stored on our servers as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your data by contacting us.

In-App Purchases

Purchases are processed by Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play). We do not collect or store any payment information.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Contact us at app.develop.sk@gmail.com
