Privacy Policy

Dotchi — Last updated: April 16, 2026

Information We Collect

Dotchi collects your votes on anime matchups and your display name if you choose to participate in community rankings. Vote data is stored on our servers to calculate and display real-time results.

How We Use Your Information

Your votes are used to calculate community rankings and display poll results. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Analytics

We use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymized usage data (screen views, feature usage) to improve the app. No personally identifiable information is collected.

Advertising

Free users see ads via Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers for ad personalization in accordance with Google's privacy policy. Premium users see no ads.

Data Retention

Vote data is stored on our servers to provide the ranking feature. You may request deletion of your data by contacting us.

In-App Purchases

Purchases are processed by Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play). We do not collect or store any payment information.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Contact us at app.develop.sk@gmail.com
