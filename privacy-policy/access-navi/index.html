Privacy Policy

Access Navi — Last updated: April 16, 2026

Information We Collect

Access Navi collects your location data to show accessible places near you. Location data is used only while the app is in use and is not stored on our servers beyond what is needed to provide search results.

Location Data

The app requires location access to find nearby accessible facilities. Location data is sent to our server to perform proximity searches and is not retained after the search is complete.

Community Contributions

When you contribute accessibility information about a place, your submitted data (accessibility details, ratings) is stored on our servers to help other users. No personal identifiers are attached to contributions.

Analytics

We use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymized usage data (screen views, feature usage) to improve the app. No personally identifiable information is collected.

Advertising

Free users see ads via Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers for ad personalization in accordance with Google's privacy policy. Premium users see no ads.

Data Retention

Community-contributed accessibility data is stored on our servers to provide the service. You may request deletion of your contributions by contacting us.

In-App Purchases

Purchases are processed by Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play). We do not collect or store any payment information.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Contact us at app.develop.sk@gmail.com
