Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I start training?
    Launch the app and tap any training category on the home screen (Single Note, Interval, Chord, etc.) or tap "Choose Training" to select your mode and difficulty.
  • What training modes are available?
    EarSense offers Single Note, Interval, Chord, Melody, and Rhythm training — each with Beginner, Intermediate, Advanced, and Expert difficulty levels.
  • How do I play a sound?
    On the training screen, tap the large play button (circle icon) to hear the audio. Then select your answer from the choices below.
  • Do I need to create an account?
    No account is required. You can start training immediately as a guest or sign in with Apple or Google to save your progress across devices.
  • How does the streak system work?
    Complete at least one training session per day to maintain your streak. Your streak is shown on the home screen.
  • Can I delete my account and data?
    Yes. Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account. This will permanently remove all your data.
  • The app crashes or won't load — what should I do?
    Try reinstalling the app. If the issue continues, please email us with your device model and iOS version.
  • How do I restore my Premium purchase?
    Go to Settings → Premium → Restore Purchases.

About EarSense

EarSense is a music ear training app that helps you develop your musical hearing through interactive exercises. Practice identifying notes, intervals, chords, melodies, and rhythms — and track your progress as you improve.
