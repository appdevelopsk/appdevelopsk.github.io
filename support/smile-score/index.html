Smile Score

Rate your smile with AI-powered analysis

How to Use

  • Take a selfie showing your smile
  • The app analyzes your smile width, symmetry, and brightness
  • View your smile score and tips to improve it
  • Share your score on social media

FAQ

  • What does the score measure? The score reflects symmetry, width, and brightness of your smile for entertainment purposes.
  • Are my photos saved? No. All processing is on-device. Photos are never uploaded.
  • Tips for a better score? Smile naturally in good lighting with your face centered in the frame.

Privacy

Smile Score does not collect or store facial images. All processing is performed locally on your device.
