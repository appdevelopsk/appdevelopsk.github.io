Face Symmetry

Discover your true facial symmetry score with AI analysis

How to Use

  • Take a selfie or import a photo from your gallery
  • The app analyzes the symmetry between left and right sides of your face
  • View your symmetry score and detailed breakdown
  • Share your results or save them to your photo library

FAQ

  • How accurate is the analysis? The app uses geometric measurements for facial landmark analysis. Results are for entertainment purposes.
  • Is my photo stored? No. All analysis is done on-device and photos are never uploaded to any server.
  • Can I retake my photo? Yes, tap the refresh button on the result screen to take a new photo.

Privacy

Face Symmetry does not collect or store facial images. All processing is performed locally on your device. We use anonymized analytics to improve the app experience.
