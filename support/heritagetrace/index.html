Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I restore my purchases?
    Go to the premium screen and tap "Restore Purchases".
  • How many UNESCO World Heritage Sites are included?
    HeritageTrace features 100 UNESCO World Heritage Sites with detailed profiles, photos, and historical information.
  • Can I use the app offline?
    Yes, all site profiles and photos are available offline once downloaded.
  • How do I cancel my subscription?
    Open the Settings app → Apple ID → Subscriptions → HeritageTrace → Cancel.
  • The app won't load — what should I do?
    Try closing and reopening the app. If the issue persists, please contact us with your device model and iOS version.

About HeritageTrace

HeritageTrace is your pocket encyclopedia of UNESCO World Heritage Sites. Explore 100 extraordinary sites across 168 countries — from ancient temples to breathtaking natural wonders.
