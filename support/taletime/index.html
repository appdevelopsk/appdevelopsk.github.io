How It Works

  • Choose a theme: Adventure, Fantasy, Science, Animals, Friendship, or Magic.
  • Name your hero and choose a hero type (child, cat, dragon, robot, princess, or wizard).
  • Take turns: Parent starts the story, child continues — the AI weaves magic between each turn.
  • Finish the story to get a complete tale you can read together again.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I start a story? Tap the "New Story" button on the home screen, choose your settings, and tap "Start Story!"
  • Who goes first? The parent always starts the story with the first turn.
  • How many stories can I create? Free accounts can create a limited number of stories. Premium unlocks unlimited stories.
  • How do I restore my purchase? Open Settings → Premium and tap "Subscribe" — your previous purchase will be restored automatically.
  • How do I add a child profile? Go to Settings → Family Profiles → Add Child.
  • How do I delete my account? Go to Settings → Delete Account. All your stories and data will be permanently removed.

Premium Subscription

TaleTime Premium is available as a monthly or yearly subscription. Unlock unlimited AI-powered stories for the whole family. Your subscription automatically renews unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. Manage or cancel your subscription in your App Store or Google Play account settings.

Privacy & Account

  • Account deletion: Go to Settings → Delete Account. This permanently removes your account and all associated stories.
  • Data stored: We store your stories, family profiles, and account information to sync across sessions.
  • Children's privacy: We do not collect personal data from children. Story content is associated with the parent account only.
