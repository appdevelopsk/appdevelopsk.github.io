Frequently Asked Questions

  • Is my grumble really anonymous? Yes. No account or personal information is required.
  • How do I delete a grumble? Long-press any grumble in your list and select Delete.
  • How many grumbles can I post? There is no limit for personal grumbles saved on your device.
  • How do I remove ads? Upgrade to Premium in the Settings screen.
