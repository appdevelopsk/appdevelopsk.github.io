Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I read offline? Episodes you've previously loaded are cached automatically for offline reading.
  • How do I customize the reader? Tap the settings icon in the reader to adjust font size, line height, and theme.
  • What is Premium? Premium unlocks all chapters, removes ads, and provides early access to new stories.
  • How do I restore my purchase? Open the Premium screen and tap "Restore Purchase."
  • How do I add to Favorites? Tap the heart icon on any work's detail page.
  • How do I delete my account? Go to My Page → Delete Account. All data will be permanently removed.

Premium Subscription

StoryVerse Premium is available as a monthly or yearly subscription. Your subscription automatically renews unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. You can manage or cancel your subscription in your App Store account settings.

Privacy & Account

  • Account deletion: You can permanently delete your account from My Page. This removes all your data from our servers.
  • Guest mode: Use the app without an account. Your reading history is stored locally.
  • Data: We store your reading history, bookmarks, and favorites to sync across sessions.
