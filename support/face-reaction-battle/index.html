Frequently Asked Questions

  • The camera doesn't detect my face. Make sure you are in good lighting and your face is clearly visible. Try the Calibration feature in Settings to personalize detection for your face.
  • How do I play Battle mode? Share a room code with a friend. Both players need to be on the same Wi-Fi or close proximity. The app uses your camera to detect the target emotion in real time.
  • My score was not submitted to the leaderboard. Ensure you have an internet connection when submitting. Enter a display name and tap Submit to appear on the global ranking.
  • The FAKE! round caught me off guard. In Solo mode, occasional FAKE! rounds announce a different emotion than the actual target. Stay sharp — it's part of the challenge!
  • How do I restore my Premium purchase? Go to Settings → Premium and tap "Restore Purchase". Make sure you are signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.
