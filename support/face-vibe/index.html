Face Vibe

Discover the mood and energy your face radiates

How to Use

  • Take a selfie or select a photo from your gallery
  • The app analyzes your facial expression and energy
  • View your vibe profile: mood, energy level, and personality traits
  • Share your Face Vibe on social media

FAQ

  • How does Face Vibe work? The app uses facial geometry analysis to detect expressions and assign vibe categories. Results are for entertainment.
  • Are my photos stored? No. All analysis is on-device and photos are never uploaded.
  • Can I get different vibes? Yes! Try different expressions or lighting for varied results.

Privacy

Face Vibe does not collect or store facial images. All processing is performed locally on your device.
