Frequently asked questions

How do I start a focus lock?
Open Sumalock, tap “Lock now” or one of the quick buttons (15/30/60 min), choose a duration and unlock method, then start. A countdown keeps you on track.

How do I unlock in an emergency?
An emergency unlock is always available — long press, type the confirmation word, or enter the parent PIN, depending on the method you chose. Every unlock is logged.

Does it fully block other apps?
On iOS, Sumalock uses a focus lock with away detection and is designed to be used together with Guided Access. On Android, a real lock of Home and Recents is available on devices set up as Device Owner via Lock Task Mode.

What does “Remove ads” buy?
It is a one-time purchase that permanently removes ads. It is not a subscription and does not auto-renew. Use “Restore purchase” on a new device.

How do I set a daily reminder?
Sumalock can send a gentle reminder at 9:00 each day once you allow notifications.
