Where Did I Put It

Never lose track of your things

Where Did I Put It helps you remember where you placed important things. Snap a photo, note the spot and a category, and search for it any time — everything stays private on your device.

Frequently asked questions

Where is my data stored?

Everything — including photos — stays on your device. There is no account and nothing is uploaded to a server.

How do I add an item?

Tap "Add item", take or choose a photo, type where you put it, pick a category, and save.

How do I find something?

Use the search box at the top, or tap a category chip to filter. You can search by name, place, or note.

How do I back up my data?

Go to Settings → Export to share a JSON backup of your saved items.

How do I remove ads?

Open Settings → Remove ads for a one-time purchase. If you reinstall, use "Restore purchase".
