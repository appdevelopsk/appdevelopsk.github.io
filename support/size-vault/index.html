Size Vault

Every family size in one place

Size Vault keeps your family's clothing, shoe, ring, hat, and school-item sizes in one tidy, private place. Add each family member, record their sizes with brand notes, and see how your children grow over time — all stored locally on your device.

Frequently asked questions

Where is my data stored?

Everything stays on your device. There is no account and nothing is uploaded to a server.

How do I record a size change?

Open a family member, tap "Add size", and enter the new value. The latest entry becomes the current size, and the previous one is kept in the size history.

How do I back up my data?

Go to Settings → Export and choose JSON, CSV, or PDF. You can then save or send the file wherever you like.

How do I remove ads?

Open Settings → Remove ads for a one-time purchase. If you reinstall, use "Restore purchase".

How do I delete everything?

Settings → Clear all data removes all members and sizes. Uninstalling the app also removes your data.
