Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I restore my purchases?
    Go to the premium/subscription screen and tap "Restore Purchases".
  • How does the Daily Challenge work?
    A new puzzle is available every day. Complete it to appear on the daily leaderboard.
  • Can I play without an internet connection?
    Some modes require an internet connection for leaderboards. Offline play is available for local modes.
  • How do I cancel my subscription?
    Open the Settings app → Apple ID → Subscriptions → Spot the Diff! → Cancel.
  • My game won't load — what should I do?
    Try closing and reopening the app. If the issue persists, please contact us with your device model and iOS version.
