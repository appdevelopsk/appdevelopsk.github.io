Frequently Asked Questions

  • How does matchmaking work?
    KeyClash matches you with players of similar skill. If no match is found in 8 seconds, you play against a bot.
  • How is WPM calculated?
    Words Per Minute = characters typed ÷ 5 ÷ minutes elapsed. Only correct characters count toward your score.
  • How do I play with a friend?
    Tap the Friend Battle tab, create a room, and share the invite code. Your friend enters the code to join.
  • Why is my rank not updating?
    Rank updates after each completed match. Bot matches do not affect your ELO rating.
