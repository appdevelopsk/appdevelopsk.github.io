Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I start recording a memory?
    Tap the "+" button on the home screen to create a new entry. You can record voice, add photos, or type text.
  • How does the AI organize my memories?
    LifeTrace uses AI to group related entries into chapters and create a timeline of your life story.
  • Is my data private?
    Yes. Your memories are stored securely and are never shared with third parties.
  • How do I sign in with Apple?
    On the login screen, tap "Sign in with Apple" and follow the prompts. Your Apple ID is used for authentication only.
  • Can I delete my account and data?
    Yes. Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account. This will permanently remove all your data.
  • The app crashes or won't load — what should I do?
    Try reinstalling the app. If the issue continues, please email us with your device model and iOS version.
