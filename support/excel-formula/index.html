Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I restore my purchase?
    Open the app, go to Settings, and tap "Restore Purchases".
  • The app is crashing. What should I do?
    Try force-closing and restarting the app. Make sure you have the latest version installed. If the issue persists, reinstall the app.
  • How do I cancel a subscription?
    Go to your device Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions (iOS) or Google Play → Subscriptions (Android), then select the app and cancel.
  • How does the OCR formula detection work?
    Point your camera at a spreadsheet formula in a book, screen, or printed material. The app will detect and parse the formula automatically.
  • What languages are supported?
    This app supports 17 languages: English, Japanese, Chinese (Simplified & Traditional), Korean, Spanish, Portuguese, French, German, Italian, Russian, Arabic, Thai, Indonesian, Turkish, Dutch, and Vietnamese.
